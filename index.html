AoC Charter for Student Engagement

Students are partners in the education process and colleges benefit enormously from engaging with them at every level. This engagement needs to be authentic and meaningful and to build on students’ knowledge, skills, experience, and aspirations.

College Commitment

  • Create and nurture a culture of partnership and engagement and treat our students as partners in learning.
  • Treat our students with respect and listen to their views.
  • Value, promote, and embed student engagement across our college's work.
  • Create inclusive student representative structures.
  • Help our students develop the skills and confidence needed for participation and leadership.
  • Encourage high participation in all student elections.
  • Promote equality, celebrate diversity, and reflect student cohort diversity.
  • Provide opportunities for student involvement in Youth Social Action and leadership activity.
